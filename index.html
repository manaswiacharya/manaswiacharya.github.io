About me

I am currently pursuing MBBS, and every day I am reminded that medicine is not only about textbooks, diagnoses, and treatment plans. It is also about listening. It is about noticing fear, hope, silence, pain, resilience, and all the small things that make a person human.

Outside medicine, I write and make art. Writing helps me understand what I cannot say out loud. Art helps me express what words sometimes fail to hold.

This site is a personal archive of that journey. Some parts will be polished. Some parts will be in progress. I like that, because I am in progress too.
